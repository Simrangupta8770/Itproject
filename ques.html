Ques 1: What are the roles of Entrepreneurship ?

The roles of Entrepreneurship are:-

It aids the transfer of Technology .
It enhances the living standards of people .
It helps in transforming the economy .
It aids the effective utilization of resources .
It creates new markets and enables expansion into global markets .

Ques 2: What is natural resource conservation.Give different ways to save and conserve the natural resources?

The protection and preservation restore ration and rational use of all the natural resources in the total environment are collectively called natural resource conservation. Some different ways to save and conserve the natural resources are tree plantation to prevent soil erosion, rainwater harvesting, use of bio fuels, recycling of waste use of energy saving fluorescent bulbs use of digital media instead of paper practice of Crop rotation use of drip irrigation, use a biogas in the kitchen ,Industrial waste and sewage treatment before they are released in the water bodies ,use of hybrid cars instead of conventional cars ,use of solar energy, wind energy and other alternative sources.

Ques 3: Define green skills.

Green skills can be defined as the skills, abilities, knowledge,and attitudes that are needed in the manpower to support economic,social,and environmental outcomes in the business industry.

Ques 4: How can enterprise be defined?

An enterprise can be defined as an entity, organisation or undertaking that is formed for commercial purposes or business ventures . The main purpose of an enterprise is to provide goods and services while considering financial, commercial and industrial aspects. It comprises people and physical assets with a common objective of generating profits.

Ques 5: What are the 3 Rs? Explain.

The 3 Rs are reduce reuse and recycle .
Reduce - minimize the use of natural resources as much as possible
Reuse - Reuse means to find new ways to use things that otherwise would have been thrown out.
Recycle - Recycle means to turn something old and useless into something new and useful
